Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, process, and protect information when you use AI Clothes Changer (the “App”).

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this policy, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect limited data strictly to provide core app functionality, improve performance, and maintain app stability.

1.1 User-Provided Photos

When you use the App to perform virtual try-ons or change outfits, you may upload photos from your device. These photos are used solely for AI-based clothing transformation and style preview purposes.

  • Photos selected from your photo library
  • Images captured using your device camera, if camera access is used

Uploaded photos are processed only for the purpose of generating the requested outfit or style result. We do not use your photos for advertising, profiling, or identity recognition.

1.2 Face Data and Processing Practices

Photos uploaded by users for virtual try-ons may contain human faces. The App processes facial features that appear in user-provided images in order to accurately align the AI-generated clothing with the user's body structure and features.

Detailed Reasons for Storing and Processing Face Data: Face data is processed strictly to perform the visual clothing transformation and "virtual try-on" requested by the user. Specifically, our AI algorithms analyze body and facial contours to ensure the generated outfit aligns realistically with the user's features for an accurate preview. This process is essential to place the garment correctly over the user's body in the photo. We do not use face data to identify or verify the identity of any individual, nor for any biometric authentication purposes.

Before any image is processed, the App informs the user and obtains explicit consent to process the uploaded photo, including any facial data that may appear in the image, for AI generation purposes.

The App does not perform facial recognition. The App does not create biometric identifiers and does not perform biometric authentication. Face data is used solely for the requested result and not for surveillance, profiling, or advertising.

Third-Party Disclosure and Privacy Practices regarding Face Data: To deliver the AI output, photos containing facial data are securely transmitted to Google Gemini / Google AI services. These third-party AI providers process the data in real-time (in-memory) to generate the visual result. Important: According to our service standards, these third parties do not store face data beyond the immediate processing time required to generate the specific image output for the user. They do not retain facial data for their own independent use, training, or extended storage. We do not share face data with any other third parties, advertisers, or data brokers.

2. AI Processing

To generate virtual try-ons, photos uploaded by users are securely transmitted to Google Gemini or related Google AI services for image analysis and AI-based result generation.

Only the user-uploaded image and the generation request are sent for this purpose. AI processing occurs only after the user explicitly selects an image, gives consent, and requests generation inside the App.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party service providers to operate and improve the App, including:

  • Google Gemini / Google AI services for AI outfit generation
  • Google Firebase for Analytics, Crashlytics, and Performance Monitoring
  • RevenueCat for subscription management

4. Tracking

The App does not use tracking technologies for advertising purposes.

  • No advertising SDKs are integrated
  • No cross-app tracking occurs
  • No App Tracking Transparency permission is requested

5. Data Sharing

We may share limited information with trusted third-party services only when necessary to operate the App:

  • User-uploaded photos, which may contain face data, are shared with Google Gemini solely to generate the requested output.
  • Firebase services receive limited diagnostics data.
  • RevenueCat receives limited subscription-related status information.

6. Data Retention, Storage and Deletion

Uploaded photos are processed temporarily for generating virtual try-on results. We do not permanently store uploaded photos on our own servers.

Retention Period (Length of Storage): All user-uploaded photos and any facial data extracted during the AI transformation process are stored temporarily.

Automatic and Permanent Deletion: To protect user privacy, all face data and uploaded images are automatically and permanently deleted from our servers within 24 hours after the processing is completed. We do not store face data indefinitely.

Specific Reason for This Storage Duration: This 24-hour window is maintained solely to ensure the technical stability of the AI generation request, allow for error handling and recovery during the generation process, and to ensure the user has sufficient time to successfully receive and download the visual result. After this brief period, all associated data is purged from our systems to ensure user security.

7. Data Security
