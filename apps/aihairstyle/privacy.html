Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, process, and protect information when you use AI Hairstyle - Hair Color (the “App”).

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this policy, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect limited data strictly to provide core app functionality, improve performance, and maintain app stability.

1.1 User-Provided Photos

When you use the App to generate hairstyle and hair color previews, you may upload photos from your device. These photos are used solely for AI hairstyle and hair color generation purposes.

  • Photos selected from your photo library
  • Images captured using your device camera, if camera access is used

Uploaded photos are processed only for the purpose of generating the requested hairstyle or hair color result. We do not use your photos for advertising, profiling, or identity recognition.

1.2 Face Data

Photos uploaded by users may contain human faces. The App may process facial features that appear in user-provided images in order to generate AI-based hairstyle previews, hair color changes, and visual hair transformations.

Reasons for Processing and Storing Face Data: Face data is processed strictly to perform the visual hairstyle and color transformation requested by the user. Our AI algorithms analyze facial contours to ensure the generated hairstyle or color aligns accurately with the user's features for a realistic preview.

Before any image is processed, the App informs the user and obtains explicit consent to process the uploaded photo, including any facial data that may appear in the image, for AI generation purposes.

The App does not perform facial recognition and does not identify or verify the identity of any individual. The App does not create biometric identifiers and does not perform biometric authentication.

Face data is used solely for generating the hairstyle or hair color result requested by the user and for no other purpose. Face data is not used for surveillance, profiling, advertising, biometric analysis, or user identification.

Uploaded images containing faces may be securely transmitted to Google Gemini or related Google AI services solely for the purpose of generating the requested image output. Third-party storage practices: These third-party AI providers process the data in real-time to deliver the visual result and do not store face data for their own independent purposes or beyond the necessary processing time required to generate the output.

1.3 Generated Images

The App may create AI-generated hairstyle and hair color preview images based on the photos and styles selected by the user. Generated images may be temporarily processed in order to deliver the requested result. Users may choose to save generated images to their own device.

1.4 Device and Usage Information

The App may automatically collect certain device and usage information.

  • App interactions (such as screens viewed and feature usage)
  • App version, device model, and operating system
  • Technical performance data

This information is used only for analytics, diagnostics, and improving the functionality of the App.

1.5 Crash and Performance Data

We collect crash logs and performance data to improve app stability and reliability.

  • Crash reports
  • Performance metrics

This information is used solely for diagnostics and technical improvements.

1.6 Subscription and Purchase Information

The App may offer subscriptions or in-app purchases managed through Apple’s App Store and RevenueCat.

We do not directly collect or store payment card details or billing information. All payments are processed securely by Apple.

We may receive limited purchase-related information such as subscription status (active, expired, canceled, or billing issue) to enable premium features.

1.7 Information We Do Not Collect

We do not intentionally collect:

  • Name, email address, or phone number unless you contact us
  • Physical address
  • Contacts
  • Precise or coarse location data
  • Payment card or banking information
  • Advertising identifiers for cross-app tracking

2. AI Processing

To generate hairstyle and hair color previews, photos uploaded by users may be securely transmitted to Google Gemini or related Google AI services for image analysis and AI-based result generation.

Only the user-uploaded image and the generation request are sent for this purpose. This data is used solely to generate the requested hairstyle or hair color output.

AI processing occurs only after the user explicitly selects an image, gives consent, and requests generation inside the App.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party service providers to operate and improve the App, including:

  • Google Gemini / Google AI services for hairstyle and hair color image generation
  • Google Firebase for Analytics, Crashlytics, and Performance Monitoring
  • RevenueCat for subscription management

These providers may process limited information according to their own privacy policies. We do not sell or rent personal data.

4. Tracking

The App does not use tracking technologies for advertising purposes.

  • No advertising SDKs are integrated
  • No cross-app tracking occurs
  • No App Tracking Transparency permission is requested

5. Data Sharing

We may share limited information with trusted third-party services only when necessary to operate the App:

  • User-uploaded photos, which may contain face data, are shared with Google Gemini / Google AI services solely to generate the requested hairstyle or hair color result
  • Firebase services receive limited analytics and diagnostics data
  • RevenueCat receives limited subscription-related status information

We do not share personal data with advertisers or data brokers.

6. Data Retention

Uploaded photos are processed temporarily for generating hairstyle and hair color results. We do not permanently store uploaded photos on our own servers.

Length of retention and why: Uploaded photos and any temporary face data are retained only for the time necessary to complete processing and deliver the result. To protect user privacy, all face data and uploaded images are automatically and permanently deleted from our systems within 24 hours. This specific timeframe ensures that the AI processing is stabilized and the user has sufficient time to receive the output. We do not store face data indefinitely.

Generated images may be saved by the user on their own device.

Analytics and crash data may be retained according to Firebase data retention policies.
