Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use AI Hairstyle - Hair Color (the “App”).

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this policy, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect limited data strictly to provide app functionality, generate AI hairstyle and hair color results, improve performance, and maintain app stability.

1.1 Photos or Images Provided by Users

The App allows users to upload personal photos in order to generate AI-powered hairstyle previews, haircut simulations, and hair color transformations.

  • User photos uploaded for hairstyle and hair color processing
  • Images generated by the AI system

Uploaded images are used solely to generate hairstyle and hair color results and provide the requested functionality. Images are processed securely and are not permanently stored unless necessary to deliver the requested result.

1.2 Device and Usage Information

The App may automatically collect certain device and usage information.

  • Device identifiers (non-advertising identifiers)
  • App interactions (screens viewed, buttons pressed, features used)
  • App version, device type, and operating system

This data is used only for analytics and app functionality purposes.

1.3 Crash and Performance Data

We collect crash logs and performance data to improve the stability and reliability of the App.

  • Crash reports
  • Performance metrics such as launch time and responsiveness

This information helps us diagnose technical issues and improve app performance.

1.4 Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect personal identity information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Physical address
  • Contacts
  • Precise or approximate location
  • Advertising identifiers used for tracking

2. Tracking

The App does not use tracking technologies for advertising purposes.

  • No advertising networks are integrated
  • No App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission is requested
  • No cross-app tracking occurs

3. How We Use Information

Collected information is used to:

  • Provide AI-powered hairstyle and hair color preview functionality

We do not sell, rent, or share user data for advertising purposes.

4. Data Sharing

We may share limited data with trusted third-party service providers used to operate the App.

These services process data according to their own privacy policies and applicable laws.

5. Data Retention

Data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide the service or as required by law. Uploaded images used for AI processing may be temporarily processed but are not stored permanently.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
