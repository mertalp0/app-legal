We collect limited data strictly to provide core app functionality, improve performance, and maintain app stability.

When you use the App to generate AI images, you may upload photos from your device. These photos are used solely for AI image generation purposes.

  • Photos selected from your photo library

Uploaded photos are processed only for the purpose of generating the requested AI result. We do not use your photos for advertising, profiling, or identity recognition.

Photos may be securely transmitted to third-party AI infrastructure in order to generate the requested image. We do not permanently store uploaded photos on our own servers unless explicitly stated inside the app for a specific feature.

1.2 Generated Images

The App may create AI-generated images based on the photos and styles you select. Generated images may be temporarily processed to deliver the requested result and may be saved by the user to their own device.

The App may automatically collect certain device and usage information.

  • App interactions (such as screens viewed, taps, and feature usage)

This data is used for analytics, diagnostics, and app functionality purposes only.

We collect crash logs and performance data to improve app stability, reliability, and performance.

  • Crash reports

This data is used solely for diagnostics and technical improvements.

We do not intentionally collect:

To generate AI images, uploaded photos may be securely transmitted to third-party AI service providers, including Google Gemini Flash or related Google AI infrastructure, solely for image generation and processing purposes.

These third-party services process the content only to provide the requested AI output. We do not use uploaded photos for advertising, resale, or unrelated profiling purposes.

We require user action within the app before photos are submitted for AI processing.
